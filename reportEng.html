02 — Main Page

index.html — Sections

The main landing page is divided into 9 themed sections, each with its own design and functionality.

Hero Section

Fullscreen banner with an editable headline, subtitle and background image. Two CTA buttons, sticky navigation bar.

Statistics Bar

4 key numbers about the kindergarten — years, children, staff, rating. Fully editable in CMS.

About Us

Two-column layout — text + photo. Two main paragraphs, extended text and a bullet list of facts, all editable.

Our Services & Activities

Dynamic activity grid loaded from Supabase. "Featured Activity" function — one activity is promoted as a prominent banner with its own description and CTA button.

Curious Bee Programme

Presentation of the educational programme. 4 pillars displayed as icon cards with editable text.

News & Updates

3-column grid of the latest articles with preview, date and colour tag. Links through to full article pages.

Tuition Pricing

Two pricing cards — full-day and half-day attendance. Fee and detail text editable in CMS.

Contact & Map

Contact info cards (address, phone, email, hours), embedded Google Map, downloadable documents, and footer with quick links.

05 — Parents' Zone

Virtual Kindergarten

A secured section exclusively for parents — accessible only with the correct password. The password is verified on the server, not in the browser.

🔐 Password verified server-side, not in the browser

Unlike common solutions, the password is not compared inside the browser (where it could be bypassed via DevTools). Verification happens exclusively through a secured database function on the server side.

Server-side verification SessionStorage — cleared on tab close RLS table protection

08 — Security

Data Protection & GDPR

Multi-layer protection of the database, parents' zone and children's personal data in compliance with the GDPR regulation.

🛡️ RLS + SECURITY DEFINER Functions

The key technical protection: even if someone copies the public anon key (visible in the source code of any static website), without the correct password they cannot retrieve any data from the parents' gallery.

skolka_get_folders(pw) skolka_get_gallery(folder_id, pw) skolka_set_password(old, new)
Security LayerDescriptionStatus
Row Level Security (RLS)Blocks direct anon key access to sensitive tables✓ Active
app_secrets tablePassword stored separately, inaccessible via REST API✓ Active
Server-side password verificationSECURITY DEFINER functions — verification on the server✓ Active
SessionStorage authenticationLogin state is cleared when the browser tab is closed✓ Active
HTTPSEncrypted communication with the server✓ Active
Cookie notice bannerGDPR notice on all pages in Slovak✓ Active
Privacy Policy (privacy.html)Full GDPR-compliant privacy policy in Slovak✓ Active
⚠️

Children's Photography — Special Category under GDPR

Photos of children are a specially protected category of personal data (Article 9 GDPR). They are accessible only after logging in to the Virtual Kindergarten. The privacy policy documents the correct legal basis — explicit consent of the legal guardian. For maximum protection, individual parent logins (each parent with their own account) are recommended as a future enhancement.
